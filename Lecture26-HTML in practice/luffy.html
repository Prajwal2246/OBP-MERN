Journey

Foosha Village (East Blue)


Luffy’s hometown where he grew up.
Met Shanks, the pirate who inspired him to become a pirate.
Received the iconic Straw Hat from Shanks.
Showed early signs of his rubber powers.
Made a promise to become Pirate King one day.

Orange Town

First major fight against Buggy the Clown.
Demonstrated Luffy’s fearless nature.
Introduced the power of his Devil Fruit abilities in battle.
First time he really protected new friends.
Marked the beginning of forming the Straw Hat crew.

Syrup Village & Baratie

Usopp joined as the sharpshooter of the crew.
Fought against Kuro’s crew to protect Usopp’s village.
At Baratie, Sanji was recruited as the cook.
Luffy proved his strength by fighting Don Krieg.
Showed Luffy’s care for his crew’s well-being.
